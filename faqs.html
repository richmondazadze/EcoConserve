FAQ

Frequently Asked

Questions

How does EcoConserve's recycling program work?

EcoConserve's recycling program is simple: collect your textile and plastic waste, ship it to us or drop it off at a partner location, and earn rewards for your contribution. We process the materials using innovative recycling technologies to minimize environmental impact.

What types of materials can I recycle with EcoConserve?

EcoConserve accepts a wide range of textiles (including clothes, shoes, and accessories) and plastics (such as bottles, containers, and packaging). We provide a detailed list of acceptable items on our website to help you recycle effectively.

How do I get paid for recycling?

Once we receive and process your recyclables, we calculate the value based on weight and material type. You can choose to receive payment via bank transfer, PayPal, or convert your earnings into gift cards from our partner retailers.

Are there any costs associated with recycling through EcoConserve?

Recycling with EcoConserve is free. If you choose to ship your items to us, you may incur shipping costs. However, we offer free drop-off at our partner locations, and for large quantities, we may arrange free pick-up services in certain areas.

How does EcoConserve ensure environmental sustainability in its processes?

EcoConserve uses state-of-the-art recycling technologies that minimize water usage and energy consumption. We also employ closed-loop systems to reduce waste and emissions. Our processes are regularly audited to ensure compliance with the highest environmental standards.
